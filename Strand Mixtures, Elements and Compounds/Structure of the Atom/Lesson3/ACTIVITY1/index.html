Activity Introduction

Electrons do not just float around randomly—they follow a special rule! Just like seats in a bus or riders in an elevator, electrons fill energy levels in order. In this game, drag electrons to the correct shell. Start from the first shell and move outward. Ready to fill the atom?

Learner Instructions

  • Drag each ⚪ electron to its correct shell.
  • The first shell holds a maximum of 2 electrons.
  • The second shell holds up to 8 electrons.
  • The third shell holds up to 8 electrons.
  • Drop electrons one at a time into each ring. Watch out for the limits!

Here's a Hint...

  • You cannot skip a shell. Fill inner shells first.
  • 1st shell → Max 2 electrons.
  • 2nd shell → Max 8 electrons.
  • 3rd shell → Max 8 electrons.
  • Example: Lithium (3 electrons) is 2 in the 1st shell, 1 in the 2nd.
