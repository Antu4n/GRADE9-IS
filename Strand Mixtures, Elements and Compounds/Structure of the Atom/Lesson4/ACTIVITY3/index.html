Activity Introduction

Get ready to build atoms! Your mission is to drag electrons into the correct shells based on the atomic configuration provided. Follow the shell filling rules: the first shell holds 2, the second holds 8, and the third also holds up to 8. Let's complete these atoms together!

Learner Instructions

  • Read the electron arrangement shown on the screen.
  • Drag electrons from the source at the bottom.
  • Drop the correct number of electrons onto each shell.
  • Click Check My Atom to receive feedback.
