Activity Wrap Up!

Activity Introduction

Put on your science detective hat! Each atom diagram shows how electrons are arranged in shells. Look closely, count carefully, and match it with the right answer.

Learner Instructions

  1. Study each dot and cross diagram of an atom carefully.
  2. Count the electrons in each shell.
  3. Choose the correct electron arrangement or element name that matches the diagram.
  4. Review the feedback to understand why your choice was correct or incorrect.
