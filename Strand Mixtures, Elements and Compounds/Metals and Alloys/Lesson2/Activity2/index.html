Activity Introduction

Alloys are not just metal mixtures, they are science-built materials! In this activity, you will explore how atoms are arranged inside pure metals compared to alloys. Click each hotspot to uncover the hidden science behind why alloys are often stronger and more useful than pure metals.

Learner Instructions

Study the diagrams of a pure metal and an alloy. Click on each hotspot to learn about that part of the structure. Read and listen to the explanation carefully. Click all hotspots to complete the activity.
